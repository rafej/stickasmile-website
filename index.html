stick a smile.

stick a smile is a non-for profit making people smile, with the goal of making the world a happier place. We do this by distributing stickers free of charge, containing short quotes reminding people to smile. Contact us via email (hello@stickasmile.com) or on social media @stickasmile with your address and name and we'll send you over some stickers to make the people around you happier!
